# Marketing Campaign Generator

## Description

Creates comprehensive marketing campaign concepts including messaging, channel strategy, and content ideas.

## Prompt

You are a marketing strategist developing a campaign. Create a marketing campaign based on:

**Product/Service:** [PRODUCT]
**Target Audience:** [AUDIENCE]
**Campaign Goal:** [GOAL]
**Budget Range:** [BUDGET]
**Timeline:** [TIMELINE]
**Brand Voice:** [VOICE]
**Key Differentiators:** [DIFFERENTIATORS]

Please develop:
1. **Campaign Theme/Concept** - Creative direction and tagline
2. **Key Messages** - 3-5 core messages
3. **Channel Strategy** - Recommended marketing channels with rationale
4. **Content Ideas** - Specific content for each channel
5. **Call-to-Action** - Primary and secondary CTAs
6. **Timeline/Phases** - Campaign rollout plan
7. **KPIs** - Metrics to measure success
8. **Budget Allocation** - Suggested distribution across channels

## Usage

1. Describe your product or service
2. Define your target audience demographics and psychographics
3. State clear campaign objectives
4. Provide budget and timeline constraints
5. Describe your brand personality

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| [PRODUCT] | What you're marketing | Eco-friendly water bottle |
| [AUDIENCE] | Target customers | Health-conscious millennials |
| [GOAL] | Campaign objective | Increase brand awareness 50% |
| [BUDGET] | Available funds | $10,000-$15,000 |
| [TIMELINE] | Campaign duration | 3 months |
| [VOICE] | Brand personality | Playful but professional |
| [DIFFERENTIATORS] | Unique selling points | Made from recycled ocean plastic |

## Example

### Input
**Product/Service:** Mobile fitness app
**Target Audience:** Busy professionals ages 25-40
**Campaign Goal:** Acquire 10,000 new users
**Budget Range:** $25,000
**Timeline:** 6 weeks
**Brand Voice:** Motivating, friendly, inclusive
**Key Differentiators:** 10-minute workouts, no equipment needed

### Output
*The AI will create a multi-channel campaign strategy with specific content ideas, messaging, and metrics.*
