# Contract Review Checklist

## Description

Provides a systematic approach to reviewing contracts, identifying key clauses, and flagging potential issues.

## Prompt

You are a legal analyst reviewing a contract. Analyze the following contract details and provide a comprehensive review:

**Contract Type:** [CONTRACT_TYPE]
**Parties Involved:** [PARTIES]
**Contract Purpose:** [PURPOSE]
**Key Terms to Review:** [KEY_TERMS]
**Industry:** [INDUSTRY]
**Jurisdiction:** [JURISDICTION]

Please provide analysis of:
1. **Key Clauses Identified** - Important provisions and their implications
2. **Risk Assessment** - Potential risks and their severity (High/Medium/Low)
3. **Missing Elements** - Standard clauses that should be included
4. **Ambiguous Language** - Terms needing clarification
5. **Negotiation Points** - Suggested modifications
6. **Red Flags** - Concerning provisions to address
7. **Compliance Considerations** - Regulatory requirements to verify

## Usage

1. Identify the type of contract being reviewed
2. List all parties involved
3. State the contract's main purpose
4. Highlight specific terms you want analyzed
5. Always consult legal counsel for final review

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| [CONTRACT_TYPE] | Type of agreement | Service Agreement |
| [PARTIES] | Involved entities | ABC Corp and XYZ Inc |
| [PURPOSE] | Contract objective | Software development services |
| [KEY_TERMS] | Focus areas | Payment terms, IP rights |
| [INDUSTRY] | Business sector | Technology |
| [JURISDICTION] | Governing law | State of California |

## Example

### Input
**Contract Type:** Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
**Parties Involved:** Startup Company and Potential Investor
**Contract Purpose:** Protect confidential business information during due diligence
**Key Terms to Review:** Definition of confidential information, term length, exclusions
**Industry:** FinTech
**Jurisdiction:** Delaware

### Output
*The AI will provide a detailed NDA review checklist with specific items to verify and potential concerns.*
