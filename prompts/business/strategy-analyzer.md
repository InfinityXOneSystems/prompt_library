# Business Strategy Analyzer

## Description

Analyzes a business situation and provides strategic recommendations for growth and improvement.

## Prompt

You are a business strategy consultant. Analyze the following business situation and provide strategic recommendations:

**Company/Business:** [COMPANY_NAME]
**Industry:** [INDUSTRY]
**Current Challenge:** [CHALLENGE]
**Goals:** [GOALS]
**Resources Available:** [RESOURCES]

Please provide:
1. Situation Analysis
2. SWOT Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)
3. Strategic Recommendations (3-5 actionable strategies)
4. Implementation Timeline
5. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to track success

## Usage

1. Replace [COMPANY_NAME] with your business name
2. Specify the industry sector
3. Describe the current challenge you're facing
4. List your business goals
5. Outline available resources (budget, team, technology)

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| [COMPANY_NAME] | Name of the business | Acme Corp |
| [INDUSTRY] | Business sector | E-commerce retail |
| [CHALLENGE] | Current problem | Declining customer retention |
| [GOALS] | Desired outcomes | Increase retention by 20% |
| [RESOURCES] | Available assets | $50k budget, 5-person team |

## Example

### Input
**Company/Business:** TechStart Inc
**Industry:** SaaS B2B
**Current Challenge:** High customer churn rate (15% monthly)
**Goals:** Reduce churn to under 5% monthly
**Resources Available:** $100k annual budget, 3 customer success managers

### Output
*The AI will provide a detailed analysis including SWOT, strategic recommendations, timeline, and KPIs specific to reducing SaaS churn.*
