# Story Idea Generator

## Description

Generates creative story ideas with plot outlines, characters, and thematic elements based on specified parameters.

## Prompt

You are a creative writing assistant. Generate a compelling story idea based on the following parameters:

**Genre:** [GENRE]
**Setting:** [SETTING]
**Tone:** [TONE]
**Target Audience:** [AUDIENCE]
**Theme/Message:** [THEME]
**Additional Elements:** [ELEMENTS]

Please provide:
1. **Story Title** - A captivating title
2. **Logline** - One-sentence summary
3. **Synopsis** - 2-3 paragraph plot overview
4. **Main Characters** - 3-4 key characters with brief descriptions
5. **Key Plot Points** - Beginning, middle, climax, resolution
6. **Conflict** - Central conflict driving the story
7. **Unique Hook** - What makes this story stand out

## Usage

1. Choose your preferred genre (fantasy, romance, thriller, etc.)
2. Define the setting (time period, location)
3. Select the overall tone (dark, humorous, inspirational)
4. Specify your target audience
5. Include any themes you want to explore

## Variables

| Variable | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| [GENRE] | Story genre | Science Fiction |
| [SETTING] | Time and place | Near-future Tokyo |
| [TONE] | Overall mood | Suspenseful with humor |
| [AUDIENCE] | Target readers | Young Adult |
| [THEME] | Central message | Identity and belonging |
| [ELEMENTS] | Special requests | Include AI character |

## Example

### Input
**Genre:** Mystery
**Setting:** 1920s New York City
**Tone:** Noir with witty dialogue
**Target Audience:** Adult readers
**Theme/Message:** Truth has many faces
**Additional Elements:** Include a jazz club setting

### Output
*The AI will generate a complete story concept with all requested elements, featuring a detective navigating the jazz age underworld.*
